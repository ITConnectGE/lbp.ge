Junior Legal Researcher

Tbilisi, Chavchavadze Full-time Deadline: June 1, 2026

About the Role

LBP — Legal & Business Partners is looking for a motivated Junior Legal Researcher to join our team. This role is ideal for recent law graduates eager to gain hands-on experience in a dynamic and fast-paced legal environment.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct legal research on Georgian and international law topics
  • Prepare memoranda, case summaries, and legal briefs
  • Assist senior lawyers in case preparation and due diligence
  • Monitor legislative developments and regulatory changes
  • Draft and review legal documents under supervision
  • Contribute to the firm's blog and knowledge-sharing initiatives

Requirements

  • Law degree from an accredited university (Bachelor's or Master's)
  • Strong analytical and critical thinking skills
  • Excellent legal writing and research abilities
  • Fluency in Georgian and English (written and spoken)
  • Familiarity with Georgian legislation and legal databases
  • Self-motivated with the ability to work independently and in a team

What We Offer

  • Mentorship from experienced legal professionals
  • Hands-on experience across multiple practice areas
  • A clear path for career advancement within the firm
  • Competitive compensation and performance-based bonuses
  • A modern office in the heart of Tbilisi at Axis Towers

How to Apply

To apply for this position, please send your resume and cover letter to info@lbp.ge
