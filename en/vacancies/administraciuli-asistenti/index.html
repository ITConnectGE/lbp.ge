Administrative Assistant

Tbilisi, Chavchavadze Full-time Deadline: June 1, 2026

About the Role

LBP — Legal & Business Partners is seeking a proactive and detail-oriented Administrative Assistant to support daily office operations and help maintain the high organizational standards our firm is known for.

Responsibilities

  • Manage daily office operations, including scheduling meetings and maintaining office supplies
  • Handle incoming and outgoing correspondence (email, phone, mail)
  • Organize and maintain physical and digital filing systems
  • Assist in preparing legal documents and presentations
  • Coordinate with external vendors and service providers
  • Support the legal team with administrative tasks as needed
  • Greet clients and visitors in a professional manner

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in business administration or a related field
  • At least 1 year of experience in an administrative or office support role
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Excellent organizational and time-management skills
  • Fluency in Georgian and English (written and spoken)
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to multitask

What We Offer

  • A professional and supportive work environment in a modern office
  • Opportunity for professional growth within the firm
  • Competitive salary commensurate with experience
  • Exposure to diverse legal and business matters
  • A collaborative team culture that values initiative and integrity

How to Apply

To apply for this position, please send your resume and cover letter to info@lbp.ge
