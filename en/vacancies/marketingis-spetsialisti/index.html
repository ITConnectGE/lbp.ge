Marketing Specialist

Tbilisi, Chavchavadze Part-time Deadline: June 1, 2026

About the Role

LBP — Legal & Business Partners is looking for a creative and results-driven Marketing Specialist to strengthen our brand presence and support business development efforts on a part-time basis.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute content strategies for social media platforms (LinkedIn, Facebook)
  • Create engaging written and visual content showcasing the firm's expertise
  • Manage the firm's website content updates and SEO optimization
  • Coordinate with lawyers to produce blog articles and thought leadership pieces
  • Monitor analytics and prepare performance reports on marketing campaigns
  • Support event planning and networking initiatives

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, or a related field
  • At least 1 year of experience in digital marketing or content creation
  • Strong copywriting skills and an eye for visual design
  • Proficiency with social media management tools and analytics platforms
  • Fluency in Georgian and English (written and spoken)
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously

What We Offer

  • Flexible part-time schedule compatible with other commitments
  • Creative freedom in shaping the firm's marketing direction
  • Competitive hourly compensation
  • Exposure to the legal industry and professional services marketing
  • A supportive team environment at a modern office in Axis Towers

How to Apply

To apply for this position, please send your resume and cover letter to info@lbp.ge
