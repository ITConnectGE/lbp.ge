Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how LBP — Legal & Business Partners ("LBP", "we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website lbp.ge (the "Site"). By using our Site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy and the preferences you set through our cookie consent banner.

2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners useful information about how their site is being used.

3. Cookie Categories

We use the following categories of cookies on our Site:

Category Purpose Can be disabled?
Necessary Essential for the Site to function properly. These include cookies that remember your cookie consent preferences and theme settings. No (Always on)
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our Site by collecting information anonymously. We use Google Analytics for this purpose. Yes

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our Site. Google Analytics uses cookies to gather data such as:

  • Number of visitors to the Site
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • How visitors arrived at the Site (e.g., from a search engine or direct link)
  • Geographic location (country/city level)
  • Browser and device technical information (technical data only, not personal information)

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It helps us improve our Site's content and user experience. Google Analytics data is processed by Google LLC. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit our Site, a cookie consent banner will appear allowing you to:

  • Accept All: Enable all cookie categories
  • Reject All: Disable all optional cookies (only necessary cookies remain)
  • Manage Preferences: Choose which optional categories to enable or disable

You can also clear your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for lbp.ge, which will cause the consent banner to reappear on your next visit.

6. Browser-Level Cookie Controls

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete individual cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being set

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of our Site and other websites you visit.

7. Google Analytics Opt-Out

In addition to managing your preferences through our cookie consent banner, you can opt out of Google Analytics across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this page periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies.
